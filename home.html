Hey guys!

My name's Sean, and I am a 23 year old software engineer from Tokyo Japan. I graduated from Northeastern University in Boston in 2024 with a BS in Computer Science and Business Administration. Now I work full time as a software engineer on the supply chain tech team at Wayfair, building high scale supply chain applications.

Outside of work, I am a coffee lover, tinkerer, and lifelong athlete and guitarist. I love being outdoors on hikes and campouts, traveling all around the world and experiencing new flavors, and playing pickup with friends or jamming with my band.

I've made it a goal of mine to stop being idle with my life outside of work, and start writing code. And a lot of it. As I build and contribute to open source projects, I will update my activities on my blog. I highly recommend you take a look at my Github in the meantime to see what I've been up to lately.
